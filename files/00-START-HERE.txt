🎮 THE 6 SYSTEMS (QUICK SUMMARY)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. BMI SYSTEM
   └─ User inputs: Age, Height, Weight
   └─ Game calculates: BMI category + daily calorie target
   └─ Personalizes: Food prices, exercise plans, character stats
   └─ Categories: Underweight → Obese (6 levels)

2. CHARACTER SYSTEM
   └─ Single student with hunger meter (0-100%)
   └─ Appearance changes with weight (90%-130% size)
   └─ 5 core stats: Health, Strength, Endurance, Intelligence, Stamina
   └─ Character evolves with health management

3. FOOD MARKETPLACE
   └─ 20+ foods in 5 categories
   └─ Buy with EXP (earned from quests)
   └─ Prices adjust by BMI category
   └─ Real-time calorie tracking

4. QUESTS & PROGRESSION
   └─ 5 daily quests (Log meals, Exercise, Balance nutrition, etc)
   └─ 50-150 XP per quest
   └─ Levels 1-50 progression
   └─ Streak tracking (7-365 day milestones)

5. COSMETICS & EQUIPMENT ⭐ NEW!
   └─ 15+ clothing sets (separate from body size)
   └─ 20+ accessories (some with stat bonuses)
   └─ Equipment with buffs (e.g., +20% XP from group sports)
   └─ Purchased with leftover EXP

6. HOLISTIC HEALTH ⭐ NEW!
   └─ Sleep tracking (7-8 hours recommended)
   └─ Fatigue meter (0-100%)
   └─ Stress management system
   └─ Life events (Exam week, sports day, vacation)
   └─ Relaxation activities (Meditate, read, play games)

BONUS: REAL-WORLD INTEGRATION ⭐ NEW!
   └─ Pedometer sync (Apple Health, Google Fit)
   └─ Barcode scanner (Scan food products)
   └─ Exercise auto-detection (Walking, running, cycling)
   └─ Health tracker integration (Wearables)
